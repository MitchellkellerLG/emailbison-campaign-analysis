## Winning Patterns & Key Insights

### What's Working:
1. **Gaming vertical dominance** - 5-7x better reply rates than other verticals
2. **Specific success metrics** - "2.1M views" vs generic "viral content"
3. **Cost comparison angle** - Positioning against expensive influencer campaigns
4. **Casual, industry-specific language** - "rocket fuel," "wishlists," "player acquisition"
5. **Pain-agitate-solve framework** - Acknowledge game quality, highlight distribution challenge
6. **A/B testing subject lines** - "4.2m eyeballs" vs "rocket fuel"

### What's Not Working:
1. **Generic SaaS messaging** - Only 1.44% reply rate
2. **Crypto/Web3 saturation** - Market seems overserved
3. **Long email sequences** - Best results from first 2 touches
4. **Vague value propositions** - "Content creation" without specifics
5. **Multi-industry campaigns** - Jack of all trades approach failing
