## Key Insights

### What's Working:
1. **Vertical-specific messaging** - Higher Ed at 6.8% shows specialization wins
2. **Specific metrics** - "140,000 students/month" more compelling than generic claims
3. **AI/LLM angle** - Timely and relevant to B2B SaaS
4. **Extended campaigns** - Best results from 3+ month campaigns

### What's Not Working:
1. **Generic positioning** - "Generic GEO" campaign failed completely
2. **Low interested conversion** - High replies not converting to interest
3. **Short campaign runs** - Many paused before reaching potential
4. **Weak CTAs** - Multiple asks dilute response
