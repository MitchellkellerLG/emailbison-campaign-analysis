## Key Insights

### What's Working:
1. **Canada outperforms US** dramatically (6.51% vs 0%)
2. **Specific case studies** (Walter Gardens) drive engagement
3. **Problem-first messaging** ("dried inkheads costing you")
4. **Concrete ROI claims** ("ROI in under 8 months")
5. **Industry-specific pain points** resonate

### What's Failing:
1. **US greenhouse campaigns** - Complete failure (0% replies)
2. **Generic messaging** without localization
3. **Technical jargon** without business impact
4. **Low interested conversion** despite replies
5. **LATAM printing** - Market mismatch

## Strategic Recommendations

### 1. GEOGRAPHIC FOCUS
- **Prioritize Canada** for all campaigns

- Test UK/Australia for English-speaking markets
