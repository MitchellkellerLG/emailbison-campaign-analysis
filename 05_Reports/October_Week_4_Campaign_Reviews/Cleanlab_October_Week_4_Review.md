### Pattern Analysis - What Works
- **Spintax variation** keeps copy fresh and avoids spam filters
- **{HOOK}** personalization placeholder drives higher engagement
- **Conversational tone** ("odd one", "btw") outperforms formal
- **Specific metrics** (98%, 99+%) more compelling than vague claims
- **Time-based value** ("weeks not months") resonates strongly
- **Question-based CTAs** feel less salesy
- **Thread replies** maintain conversation continuity
- **Short, punchy paragraphs** improve readability

### Pattern Analysis - What Doesn't Work
- **Overly long Step 2 emails** - Some follow-ups are too dense
- **Generic placeholders** like {SL} instead of actual subject lines
- **Incomplete variants** (e.g., "g" as email body) hurt testing
- **Missing social proof** in later follow-ups reduces credibility
- **No clear next steps** in some sequences
