2. **Hook Strategy**:
   - Personalized hooks based on company research
   - Focus on indoor growing and yield improvement
   - Examples: "Are you guys doing any indoor growing atm?"

3. **Value Proposition**:
   - Increased propagation for higher yields
   - Elimination of wetting-related root rot & mold
   - Case study reference: Walter Gardens 65,000 sq ft facility
   - Payback period: 6-12 months

### Digital Print Campaign
**Key Messaging Elements**:
1. **Pain Points Addressed**:
   - Costly ink head replacements
   - Refeed issues
   - Print quality problems

2. **Solution Focus**:
   - Precise humidity control (±3% RH)
   - No wetting/misting
   - >$100k annual maintenance savings

**Key Messaging Elements**:
1. **Technical Approach**:
   - Humidity inconsistencies causing pick-and-place errors

2. **Technology Highlight**:

   - Non-wetting dry fog system
