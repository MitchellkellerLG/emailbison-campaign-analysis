### 2. Free High-Value Deliverables (Foundation)

**Performance:** 7.19% reply rate, **33.6% interested rate**

#### What Works

**Campaign #209: Free Reddit SEO Audit**
- 113 unique replies
- **38 interested leads** (highest absolute count)
- 33.6% conversion rate (reply â†’ interested)

#### The Winning Formula

**Step 1: Quick, Easy Yes**
> "Would it be okay if I sent you a free audit that covers your current opportunities on Reddit?"

**Key Elements:**
- "Free" (removes friction)
- "Audit" (specific deliverable, not vague)
- "Your current opportunities" (personalized)
- Low commitment ask (yes/no question)

**Step 2: Value Clarification**
> "The report shows Reddit traffic opportunities based on searches you currently rank for. It'll tell you what subreddits to target and posts to make to generate conversions."

**Key Elements:**
- Concrete deliverables (subreddits, post types)
- Based on their data (personalized)
- Outcome-focused (conversions, not vanity metrics)

**Step 3: FOMO + Social Proof**
> "Ever since the Googleâ€“Reddit partnership and GEO beginning to take over, Reddit has become the leading platform for product-led searches. 97% go to Reddit to make that final choice."

**Key Elements:**
- Timely trigger (partnership news)
- Scarcity ("easy to capture right now")
- Stat-backed FOMO (97%)

#### Why It Works

**Psychological Triggers:**
1. **Reciprocity** - Free value creates obligation to engage
2. **Specificity** - "Reddit audit" > "SEO audit" (more tangible)
3. **Proof of expertise** - Offering audit demonstrates capability
4. **Low friction** - No demo, no call, just value

### ðŸš€ Campaign Launch Sequence

**Week 1: Research & Setup**
1. Identify specific audience (100-1000 contacts)
2. Research unique pain points
3. Define free value deliverable
4. Create contextual hook

**Week 2: Copy & Technical**
1. Write 2-3 step email sequence
2. Create 10-20 spintax variations
3. Set up plain text, tracking OFF
4. Verify email list (<5% bounce)

**Week 3: Launch & Monitor**
1. Launch at 200-300 emails/day
2. Monitor reply rate daily
3. Calculate interested rate after 20+ replies
4. Adjust if reply rate <3% after 100 sends

**Week 4: Optimize**
1. Analyze interested rate (target: >30%)
2. Identify winning messages
3. Pause underperformers
4. Scale winners
