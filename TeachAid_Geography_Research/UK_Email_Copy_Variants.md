### Key Differences Between Variants

- **Variant B**: Teacher workload crisis and retention

### UK-Specific Elements Used
- **Head Teacher** (not Principal)
- Ofsted and Intent/Implementation/Impact framework
- National Curriculum and Key Stages
- Supply teachers (not "substitute teachers")
- MAT (Multi-Academy Trust) and LA (Local Authority)
- Primary and secondary (not "elementary and high school")
- 60-hour weeks, 50% leave within 5 years statistics
- London, Manchester, Birmingham references

### UK Terminology Used
- "Maths" NOT "math"
- "Years" NOT "grades"
- "Pupils" in primary context
- Supply teachers
- Head teacher
- Trust/LA

- Short subject lines
- "weird one" opening
- "Also {FIRST_NAME}"
- Simple question pattern
- P.S. credibility formula
