# EmailBison MCP Server

## Purpose
Access and manage email campaigns, sequences, leads, and automation features through the EmailBison service.

## What It Does
EmailBison MCP provides comprehensive email automation and campaign management tools for:
- **Campaign Management**: Create, update, pause, resume, archive, and duplicate campaigns
- **Email Sequences**: Build multi-step email sequences with timing and personalization
- **Lead Management**: Create, update, tag, and organize leads
- **Conversation Tracking**: View and analyze email conversations and replies
- **Email Account Management**: Manage IMAP/SMTP sender accounts
- **Blacklist Management**: Control blacklisted emails and domains
- **Workspace Management**: Switch between and manage multiple workspaces
- **Analytics & Reporting**: Track campaign performance and engagement

## Key Features
- 54+ specialized tools for email automation
- Multi-workspace support
- Tag-based organization for campaigns, leads, and email accounts
- Bulk operations for leads and blacklists
- Custom tracking domains
- Webhook integrations
- Campaign scheduling and calendar automations
- Conversation thread tracking
- GDPR-compliant data handling

## Common Use Cases
- B2B cold email outreach campaigns
- Automated follow-up sequences
- Lead generation and management
- Sales pipeline automation
- Email deliverability management
- Multi-client campaign management (via workspaces)
- Performance analytics and reporting
